Why Donate Blood?

  • Reduce risk of heart disease

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

  • Age: 18–65 years
  • Weight: minimum 50 kg
  • Hemoglobin: minimum 12.5 g/dl
  • No major illness
  • No recent tattoos or piercings
