Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How often can I donate blood?

You can donate whole blood every 56 days (8 weeks). Platelet donations can be made every 7 days, up to 24 times per year.

What are the eligibility criteria?

You must be 18–65 years old, weigh at least 50kg, have a hemoglobin level of 12.5g/dl or higher, and be in good health.

How long does donation take?

The entire process takes about 45–60 minutes, including registration, health screening, donation (8–10 minutes), and recovery time.

Is blood donation safe?

Yes, blood donation is completely safe. We use sterile, single-use equipment and follow strict safety protocols.

How do I request blood urgently?

Call our 24/7 emergency helpline at +91 123 456 7890 or fill out the urgent blood request form on our website.

Can I specify where my blood goes?

While you cannot specify individual recipients, you can indicate preferences for local hospitals or emergency reserves.
